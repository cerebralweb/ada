Episode 2: Pablo Ordoñez

Episode 2 of SmallData - the Hexis podcast - is dedicated to chatting with Juan Pablo Ordoñez, Marketing Director for brands such as Pepe Ganga and Dafiti.The first strong point in the conversation was the comparison made by Juan Pablo about the differences between a company like Pepe Ganga and a (once) startup like Dafiti. The first has a lot of "traditional" compared to the second. Pablo was impressed, for example, by the enormous attention that Pepe Ganga was given to the use of uniforms and badges, while at the same time there were difficulties in understanding issues such as the advent of electronic commerce.
