International courier in Bogotá and shopping experience

Paulo César León Palacios

In this article ❧ In this article ❧ We made a comparison between the DHL and Servientrega services in Bogotá. The two brands compete for the messaging market, especially in the Millennial segment

International messaging is essential for any Millennial living in a city. Security, traceability and speed are priorities that are above cost in the mind of a typical Millenialcitizen . Who use the international messaging in this segment are people of middle or upper class, with university education and, Frequently, they have used this service several times, with different providers and even in different countries.

Furthermore, messaging is increasingly seen as a fundamental element of working life: its good operation depend contracts, commercial transactions, publications and a long etc. In the era from the, moreover, messaging has leapt from the old concept of travel to the contemporary concept of instant. .

What is instant for Millennials?

In the minds of many users of this segment the instantaneous is associated with deliver difficult things on a promise of value:

  1. Transparency in information
  2. Synchronization between the value proposition and the sale
  3. Immediate understanding of customer needs

In this ethnography we analyze the problem of how to achieve a magical shopping experience in international courier services.

Segments mentioned in this article?

men, medium income, college age 25-38 years, young entrepreneurs or employees of large companies

A Good service is hard to find

I stood in the left row because it was closer to the information desktop. On my right two people were paying a shipment to Medellín; I was relieved, because the fact that there were two rows and two persons (two women) attending gave the impression of being in the middle of a safe shopping experience.

The woman who attended my row had the company uniform, although with a certain informality; and I couldn't avoid to notice that her blouse (the company blouse) was unbuttoned enough to show a second blouse and a chain of silver. The line advanced. This is going to Mexico City , I said, as I took a package of sheets that weighed roughly 1 kilo and a half (as I found out three days later when I went to DHL). The employee did not hesitate: How much do we ask for the insurance policy? she asked her partner on the other side. She answered: for 5 thousand , but after thinking about it for a moment remembered: It's 50 thousand! . I was surprised that they passed from one number to the other like that.

They didn't weigh the package, either. Instead they ensured that they had to go through a shipment of merchandise of two kilos. Why? , I asked. It is that we are intermediaries . Although I did not see the relationship between both things I hastened to ask: What company do you ship it with? . Oh, I don't remember the name , said the 50 thousand woman .

So... but I need to know which company to send it to; it is a very important package for me.

Let me call on someone, because I don't remember.

Well.

And ... can the guide be tracked online?

No. It is that we are intermediaries.

...

She dialed and did like they did not answer; I was waiting; she continued with the gestures, as if she were calling on a friend and not someone in the company. It is not just any company. I will not say the name, but it is a long-standing organization, thousands of employees, capital, branding, marketing. By the way, the local was an official premise.

Last detail: the shipping price would be 250 thousand Colombian pesos.

I left the premises because I was afraid of the security of my package (the corrections of a book that I will publish in Mexico.)

I toured the mall. I did not find another courier company. I came back. The employees still remembered me. They gave me a photocopy to fill out with the data. As I filled it with resignation, I asked again for the name of the company that did the international shipping and whether they could track the guide online. I don't know , No , is that we are intermediaries , were the answers. Although I entered the second time willing to pay, I turned to go. That was it.

A Good Service is Easy to Find

Everything was consistent at the DHL premise. Even though I was an hour from my house, the subjective value of my package me convinced to make the trip; I was also convinced that the price would be a little lower, since I had used before the services of that brand. But I did not imagine that the savings would be 110 thousand pesos (and, in addition, it would have a number of guide and the assurance that my package would be at its destination two days later); I was also surprised that I was allowed record and ask research questions.

In a country where concern for security is very high, I found myself faced with a paradox: this company of courier services offered me all the security in the world without use security as an excuse to stop my curiosity. Consumer participation in an environment of transparency is plus in our times, since is happening an evolution from a passive consumer - buyer - towards an active consumer, who assumes the brand as a community of which it is a part.

What Makes us Buy

Some outstanding observations emerge here.

  1. The meaning of a customer question goes beyond the question: As buyers, when we ask something we are expressing an emotional need for security and order: whoever achieves satisfying that does not win a purchase, gains affection, accompanied by ideas; those ideas, moved by those affections spread in places like this.
  2. Translated into the courier business, this tells us that at conquering a segment like Millennials of middle class the price could no so important. An international shipment is a shipment of a piece of a someone's life . The user wants to be reflected in the way the service is provided , even if it implies making an extra effort to buy.
  3. Where's the magic in this case? one, educated employee, two, convincing staging, three, coherence between what is promised and what is delivered.
  4. In the messaging business, instant transparency is essential.
  5. At DHL there was an employee; in the other two: invest in technology, logistics and experience results in comparative advantages over companies that spend too much on low-skilled employees, but not take advantage of existing technology platforms.
  6. With this insight the national brand would gain in sales quickly: the proof is that I myself had paid 60% more if they had the right value proposition (even with a better communication would have been enough).
