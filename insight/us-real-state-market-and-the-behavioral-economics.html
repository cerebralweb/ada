Using Psychology to Understand the Purchase of an Apartment

Paulo César León Palacios

In this article ❧ In this article we present a case about the real estate market. These are two families with whom we developed a qualitative research to observe consumer sentiments on real estate credit

HAVING AN APARTMENT is an essential concern of every middle-class Latin American citizen. Nowadays more than 70 % of the region's population lives in cities with more than one million inhabitants, and at least 50% are paying for a home or are in the process of acquiring it. The cost of real estate, however, is it so high that horizontal property is practically the only solution for the urban middle classes in the region.

In this article we present a case study. These are two families with whom we developed a qualitative investigation whose objective was to observe the feelings of the consumer of real estate credit.

The main obstacle that the probable buyer of this type of credit faces is himself. Impulses of consumption that a person has daily can end up sabotaging (or precipitating) the acquisition of a real estate credit .

The markets set certain conditions such as the price of credit or the shortage of potential buyers, but it is the probable buyer who somehow decides how much of her income will go to that ambiguous object that is the a savings account. How do consumers who need real estate credit get around this problem? With what Practices of consumption does savings collide? How to value the experience of saving from the point of view of financial services and real estate?

AT A DISTANCE Fredy looks like any merchandise dealer. Every morning he gets up at 1am for have breakfast, put on a white butcher suit and drive a van full of raw chickens. It ends at noon. By that time he has already distributed more than three hundred kilos of breasts and legs throughout the city. The last stop is at my house. On one occasion I intercepted him with an unusual request: "I just saw that you supply the chicken to Doña Fulanita's store, would you mind to sell it directly to me? ". His first reaction was to tell me that Doña was going to be upset at that, but then - as I asked for his card, aware that exchanging cards is key in any business - he thought about it and answered "we look there".

Sometimes he travels with the oldest of his daughters, either because he is on vacation or because he has picked her up at the school. She is a girl of about 11 years; It isn't gonna take long to go to university. In its profile picture on Whatsapp I see that, in addition, he has another girl, a little younger, and a wife.

It seems to me a miracle that he doesn't fall asleep while driving, but - according to him - has become accustomed to sleep during the day , contrary to what most human beings do. But it's going well. I want to think so.

I think Fredy, his dotters and his wife are a middle class family. They have a business as prosperous as possible, a Reasonably large family, vehicle, and premises. So it is not strange that they want to acquire a new house . The apartment in which they live has just two bedrooms and is in a noisy and unwelcoming area.

Despite the small and fragile prosperity, he is not yet a candidate for a loan. real estate . First of all, he has not finished to pay the mortgage of his current apartment, and the proven income - and the fact that it originates from a small business - does not allow him to achieve the score necessary for the amount that would be required if he wants to buy a small house or a bigger apartment (more than double than his current apartment worth).

The answer of Davivienda appreciated his request to "operate" a credit with the brand, and alluded to the "investigation" of an "accounting department" that had made the technical decision. In the third paragraph they invited to invest the savings in a CDT. There the letter ended.

That was in November. In December Fredy decided that they were going on vacation to the Beach. He had not forgotten that weeks before he had agreed with his wife to continue saving, waiting patiently the time for a new request to Davivienda . Nor had he forgotten that several months ago they had been going out on Sunday expeditions with his family looking for notices of for sale.

Those tours always ended with a list that included various places promoted by RV , a well-known real estate firm in the capital. In one of the appointments with an agent of that company found a house whose price and conditions made them excited. The following Monday, Fredy and his wife began the request to Davivienda.

Fredy had not forgotten anything. It only occurred to him that the start of the new saving cycle could be postponed for a few months; after the holidays.

  • The "sale" of a property is considered to begin when the buyer makes an offer, and not when the consumer is developing the process of saving and exploring the market.
  • What is not "sale" is covered with physical or virtual publications, which are not subject to systems of high quality.
  • There is no research on how to use technology beyond posting notices on websites. For example, few real estate they develop apps, an option that could have an impact at low cost, let's say in the area of ​​pedagogy for acquisition of real estate.
  • The same goes for banks, which do invest in software development but only for administration of portfolios: there is no perceived potential value in offering educational services for potential users.
  • The real estate market is profitable in the present, as it works: it is assumed that in the future it will continue being profitable as if the current game conditions could not change.

AT her 49 YEARS OLD Elsa still lives with her mother. "No one has ever thought about me as a single woman --she says--, because I had my first daughter when I was 17". Then she had two other girls with her current boyfriend, a man twenty older that is married (and not with her, in case there are doubts).

The house in which she lives is part of a large house built in the 1960s by her father, an former worker of Bavaria, a very well known Colombian company. "I don't know why he built it with rooms that communicate with each other but not with the hallway. It is like if he thought that some of her daughters were going to need the room for living - Explains - "But these are not apartments. They do not have a bathroom, kitchen or separate exits." Elsa has felt as protected as lacking privacy . Her mother controls - without any drama - her schedules, friendships and raising their girls; It is as if instead of being three daughters there were four.

It is amazing what "owning" a house can mean for a person. Elsa was willing to pay it in 20 or 25 years: it would be completely hers only in her last years . In exchange for sharing with the bank the legal ownership would the property of her life and the direction of her family be in her for the first time. That's why I wanted the credit.

When the BBVA bank approved her request, she felt an unknown happiness . The experts in psychology of consumer say that it is almost impossible to determine the degree of satisfaction of a person, but the gesture that Elsa did while she was telling me really transmitted to me the uniqueness of that event in her life.

"I stopped going on vacation so long that I lost count of the years." I wonder how he did Elsa to postpone the little perks of so many vacations to focus on the infinite patience that is needed to achieve the goal: not the home ownership: a credit. Incidentally, describing this willingness to save as "infinite patience" is not pure romanticism, but a way to put into words a particularly arduous experience of consumption.

Her family context and gender are essential to understand her consumption preferences. In the Research on microeconomics and behavioral economics has prevailed the idea that women tend to be less risky than men to make economic decisions . That, without a doubt, is one of the causes.

Elsa represents herself as "a female head of a family", who has a partner with whom she cannot form a conventional family. So, somehow, she feels like she had to try "twice as hard." That perception also helped her to defer satisfying more immediate consumption impulses that would have deviated her from the strategic consumption goal .

Insights

  • Our study shows that some people will behave emotionally, rather than rationally, at the time to make financial consumption decisions. But why? Some people drop out the idea of ​​obtaining a loan in a close period of time because, despite fulfilling with almost all the requirements: they simply do not have a sufficient financial education .
  • Two: gender matters. Certain Financial decisions are understood by examining the identity that people have built around what means being a man or a woman. Many clients make decisions following more or less unconscious scripts: a man would pay this way or that way, a woman would handle money in this or that way.
  • Three: the credit market would benefit from programs to promote credit practices like team work in families, so families make more rational decisions and are more consistent over time. The perception of savings that users have could also be the object of financial education by banks and real estate
