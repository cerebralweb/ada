This is The Latin Business Path Project





What The LBP Project is? (1st draft)

We research to understand entrepreneurship in Latin America . Nowadays, figuring out trajectories of people involved in business has become an essential subject: first, businesses managers and entrepreneurs have got that markets and sales are about human to human relationships; second, it has been discovered that even in huge organizations clerks and middle management positions have a lot to do with businesses results.

That's why we invented The Latin Business Path Project. We research businesses people experiences

through their professional life. Now we come up with a first definition: In business researching Experience is related to the interactions between personal history, emotions, environment and business decisions.



Colombians, Mexicans, Brazilians, Hispanics in the United States, all these populations are represented in the businesses world and all of them have things to tell. We're going to find it out and show you.




Our Goals

Community Goals

We're going to share on the Internet every meaningful piece of information, and we want to make it in an interesting way.



The first thing we want to do is share this website, as well as the Linkedin and Patreon websites of this project.



The second thing: we will publish excerpts of the interviews as videos. However, our Patrons will have former access to some material.



Researching Goals

We have to make contact with as many persons as possible in order to capture and deliver as many stories as possible.



Hundreds of Skype and personal interviews should be conducted.



Scientific Goals

At the moment, we do not pursue quantitative validity, but rather the deepest insights in the business life; whereas, quantity always helps to run into interesting things. In the end, the way we observe reality is the definitive condition.



The project covers people born between 1978 and 2000 . In other words, we are interested on millennials. Why? Because they are the more active population in the business world, and they're going to take off the elder cadres very soon.



We going to publish at least one scientific paper exposing the results of the research, in a peer review journal . Our Patrons will have the right to participate in the discussions about, and even take part in fun workshops --free of boring academic stuff but plenty of food--.





Who we are?

Carmen Zavala Ramírez

Born in México, Carmen is a specialist in cultural studies and published a book and some scientific papers on the relationship among health, consume and culture. She teaches at the Rosario University, in Bogota, in the Medicine School. Usually she is in charge of anthropology and sociology courses related with health and illness. She landed a PhD in 2015 in The College of Mexico.

Academic website: https://colmex.academia.edu/CarmenZavala



Paulo César León Palacios

Paulo is a Colombian social scientist, focus on sociology, cultural history and qualitative researching. He's the author of one book and 14 peer reviewed papers about sociology of industry, political culture in Latin America and cultural consumes. He got his PhD in 2013 and was Posdoctoral Fellow at The University of Chicago (Center For Latin American Studies). He has been professor in the National University, Caldas University and Rosario University, in Colombia.
