Hexis Podcast Episode 1

Episode 1 of SmallData , the Hexis podcast, is dedicated to chatting with Juan Sebastián Gómez, Colombian developer specialized in mobile applications and Informatic security. In the interview, Sebastián narrates his beginnings as a developer of video games (as part of his undergraduate thesis), and how that led him to work with computer security companies. In that context, he got involved in some startups, like Preo (BBC).

The interview was conducted in Spanish
