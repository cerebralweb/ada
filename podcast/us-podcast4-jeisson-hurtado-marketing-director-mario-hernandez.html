Episode 4: Jeisson Hurtado

Episode 4 of SmallData - Hexis' podcast - is dedicated to chatting with Jeisson Hurtado, marketing director at Mario Hernández, the number one Colombian brand of leather products. Jeisson studied marketing and advertising on the Politecnico Gran Colombiano and before working on Mario Hernández led marketing processes at Carrefour and Cencosud (in the latter one he spent 6 years and led digital marketing development).

Interview spoken in Spanish.
